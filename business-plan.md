## Executive Summary

**1st Base** is an AI education brand built by Will Sanchez (Vancouver, WA). Will teaches regular people how to use AI through practical lessons, short-form content, curated resources, and direct help when they need it. The business has two sides: a creator/education brand (this plan) and a product/software side that supports the brand over time.

**One-liner:** I teach people how to use AI.

## Problem

Most AI content is made for developers or hype-chasers. Regular people — who actually want to use these tools in their work and life — get left behind. The tutorials are too technical. The tools feel overwhelming. Nobody's showing them in plain English.

---

## Solution

1st Base is the resource for regular people learning AI:
- Tutorials that show, not just tell
- A community where they can ask questions and share what they're building
- A newsletter that filters the noise and gives them one useful thing a week

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:** General tech/AI enthusiasts. Not developers. People who use Google, YouTube, and ChatGPT and want to go deeper. 25-45, some college, working professionals or self-employed.

**Secondary:** Small business owners who want to use AI to save time but don't know where to start.

**Not:** Enterprise, developers, researchers, or AI insiders who already know this stuff.

# 1st Base AI — Roadmap

> Source of truth for current priorities.
> Last updated: 2026-04-26

## Current Priorities

### 1. Content Engine
- Publish `3-5x` Shorts/Reels every week
- Publish `1x` YouTube video every week
- Keep a visible pipeline from idea → drafted → ready to record → published
- Turn AI news and tool changes into plain-English content for regular people

### 2. Learning Basics
- Keep studying Claude, Codex, and Gemini in simple focused sessions
- Turn technical notes into beginner-friendly lessons
- Build a repeatable habit of "learn it, simplify it, teach it"

### 3. Website + Newsletter Foundation
- Keep `website/content.md` as the source of truth for live site copy
- Keep the website docs aligned with the current graphite/cream/blue/yellow brand system
- Tighten the value proposition so regular people understand 1st Base instantly
- Keep Beehiiv/newsletter aligned with weekly content and lessons

### 4. Community + Lesson System
- Build simple lessons for Skool/community content
- Organize lessons around beginner wins, not advanced theory
- Make every lesson easy to repurpose into Shorts, YouTube, and newsletter copy

### 5. Ops + Automation
- Keep the AI Digest workflow documented and useful
- Track business crons, recurring tasks, and reminders in one place
- Use automation to reduce scatter, not create more systems to babysit

### 6. App Development Stays Secondary
- App/product ideas can support the business
- They do not outrank content creation, learning basics, or publishing rhythm
- Only work on app stuff after the weekly content and learning priorities are handled
